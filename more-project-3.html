Project Title: I Love My Body, I Hate My Body


Description:


For this project, we were asked to identify something (anything) that we love but also hate just as much if not more. The goal of the project was to see the juxtaposition of this love and hatred side by side, and view what those feelings would communicate to each other in the same spread. I decided to explore a very sensitive topic for my subject: my own body.


This project really put me into a position of criticism, not only as an artist, but as the critic I have always been towards myself. Through the exploration of my insecurities, I was able to find the beauty in whatever I may consider flawed, helping me see myself in a better light. I hope I can inspire others to do the same.
