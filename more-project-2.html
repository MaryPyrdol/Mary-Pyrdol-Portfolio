Project Title: A Significant Location


Description:


A significant location I have been exposed to all my life is Walt Disney World. For this project, I aimed to capture the tradition of magic and entertainment with my interpretation of the things I may encounter on every trip to the amusement park. What I found interesting about this project was my analysis of my perspective of Walt Disney World from different ages. I remember as a kid, the park felt like an immersive experience, and the magic felt real. Now as an adult, I am still able to experience the magic by appreciating the tradition and evolution of Disney media.


In my collection, you'll find different textures, abstractions, and interpretations of various visual inspirations across the Walt Disney World Parks.
