Project Title: Shakespeare Out Loud

Description:

A collection of posters featuring different plays, all relating to one another.

I wanted to convey that the posters were "siblings of the same family", what with their similar overall features. Some posters are the inverted colors of others, some are inverted text manipulations of each other. And yet, with the stark contrast of the loud color palette, I was able to allow the colors to communicate with one another to make them feel connected.
