Project Title: From Speech to Song

Description:

This is my typographical animation of the sermon intro of Let’s Go Crazy by Prince and The Revolution. For this typography project, I was tasked with animating a time-based narrative, anything from ____ to ____. I came up with “from speech to song”, and tried to sync up the rhythm of this song with flashing colors.


DISCLAIMER:

Bright flashing lights may be sensitive to some viewers, viewer discretion is advised.
