About

Hey there!

My name is Mary and I am a visual strategist, among many other titles. I call myself a visual strategist because I feel it best describes my passion: I want to use my creative lens to improve the quality of life. Whether that means designing an app to share cooking recipes or designing an app to help bird owners care for their beloved avian friends, I find joy in creating unique tools to help people navigate everyday life. It is my strategic approach to connecting with people through graphic design.

Since delving deep into fields like user experience design and web development, I have discovered that, yes, I am designing for the “user” of my product, but I am also designing for the person beyond the screen. I now possess this ability to sculpt technology into more human-centered experiences, and that is what inspires me about the design field. This means asking important questions like, How can I make this task easier with graphic design? or How can I connect with people using graphic design?

Since my graduation from Boston University in May of 2023, I have been exploring my passion for user experience design as well as product design by doing freelance design and earning my professional UX certification from the Google UX Professional Design Certificate through Coursera. I have also had the pleasure of sharing my appreciation for interactive design as an adjunct professor of graphic design and web programming at Lesley University's College of Art and Design in Cambridge, MA.

These days, I am eager to get involved in new graphic design opportunities, so I encourage you to connect with me if you are interested in getting to know me better! Feel free to explore my portfolio here or my art Instagram page to get a glimpse of what I do best.
