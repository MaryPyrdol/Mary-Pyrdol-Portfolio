Project Title: Acknowledgements

Description:

“Acknowledgements” highlights what society is lacking when considering the history of this land. For my project, I chose to acknowledge the origins of the Charles River, initially given the name Quinobequin, meaning “meandering”. The colors I use in my poster aim to represent the detriment of water quality over time, as well as including actual research from my 12th grade science fair project about ocean acidification. I've always been invested in environmental justice, and I felt that this was an appropriate way to consider the perspective of the Massachusett tribe witnessing the deterioration of an ecosystem they respect and may worship.
